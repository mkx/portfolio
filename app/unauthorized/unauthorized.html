Portfolio

Welcome to this portfolio app!

This is a pure Web/JavaScript application to monitor performance and other key indicators of your stock portfolio. It uses data stored in your Google Drive.

Sign in now

Technologies

This application is written in JavaScript using a suite of available libraries, mainly AngularJS and D3.js.

Stock quotes

It uses community provided tables in Yahoo Query Language to check your stock's performance against todays prices. Please use responsibly and keep in mind their terms.

Try!

It is easy to run this application. Just copy this Google Spreadsheet into your Drive and Sign in.

Privacy

It is written with a strong focus on the safety and privacy of your data. Hence, it does not store any portfolio data. All you need to do is provide a Google Spreadsheet like this one.

Improve!
